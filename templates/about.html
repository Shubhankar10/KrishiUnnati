About Us

"कृषि Unnati" is a website created by the team FARMbit to help the farmers benefit in agricultural sector & to provide personal attention to individual farmers.Through the web app we provide online teleconsultation services as well as offline alternatives to farmers.They can get support in various agricultural activities, by the help of consultants as well as connect with their peers through the web app. This website has been created as a to DRDO’s problem statement for Smart India Hackathon 2022.
